Network and Connections

Why Networking Matters

Building a strong professional network is essential for career growth and opportunities. Networking can help you:

  • Discover job and internship opportunities
  • Gain insights into different industries and roles
  • Receive advice and mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Build relationships that can support your career development
